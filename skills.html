Workflow

How I usually approach the work

01 Discover

Understand the page and its role

I start by finding the gaps in the current experience: what feels unfinished, what lacks hierarchy, and what would help a visitor understand the work faster.

02 Design

Create clearer visual systems

Then I build out stronger sections, cards, spacing, and calls to action so the interface looks more intentional and easier to navigate.

03 Build

Implement with polish

I focus on responsive behavior, readable structure, and enough interaction detail to make the experience feel finished rather than simply functional.
